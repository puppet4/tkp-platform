# SLA / SLO Policy (Phase 4)

## External SLA
- Availability SLA: 99.9% monthly.
- Critical ticket response: within 30 minutes.
- High severity response: within 4 hours.

## Core SLO
- Ingestion failure rate <= 10%.
- Retrieval p95 latency <= 3000 ms.
- Retrieval citation coverage >= 95%.

## Breach Handling
- Open incident ticket within 10 minutes.
- Trigger alert dispatch and assign owner.
- Publish recovery ETA and postmortem summary.
